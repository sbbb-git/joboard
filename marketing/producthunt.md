# ProductHunt launch

## Pre-launch (1 week before)

1. Schedule launch for **Sunday or Tuesday** EU evening (12:01 PST = 21:01 Paris) — PH "days" run on Pacific time. Sun/Tue have the lowest competing volume.
2. Set up your maker profile with avatar, bio, X/LinkedIn links.
3. Add 5-8 hunters you've previously upvoted to your "Subscribers" — they get notified.
4. **Don't pre-launch ask for upvotes**. PH explicitly forbids this and downranks for it.

### Description (≤ 260 chars)

```
slateremote.com aggregates remote tech jobs from eight public job board APIs
into one fast searchable index. Free, no signup, no resume parsing. Six
thousand pages of jobs, salaries, cities and skills. Available in English,
French, Spanish, German, Portuguese, Italian and Polish.
```

### First comment from the maker

```
Hey PH 👋

I built slateremote.com because I was tired of remote-jobs aggregators
that either nag you for a signup, parse your resume, or hide listings
behind a paywall. It's a free, opinionated index of remote tech roles
pulled directly from the public APIs of eight job boards every 24 hours.

What it does:
• Filters jobs by role, country, seniority, employment type, and salary
  band — all client-side, no signup.
• 6,800 indexed pages including job details, role salary bands, city
  guides, and 75+ long-form guides on landing and sustaining a remote
  tech career.
• Native in 7 languages with proper hreflang, not auto-translated junk.

Built on Next.js 14 static export + Cloudflare Pages. Daily GitHub
Action handles the scrape and redeploy. No client database, no auth,
no tracking beyond a privacy-friendly first-party analytics beacon.

Honest about the business: a handful of guide pages include affiliate
links (Wise, Beehiiv, AppSumo, Fiverr) marked rel=nofollow sponsored.
That covers domain + CDN costs. Browsing and the index itself stay
free, forever.

Happy to answer anything technical (the i18n setup is fun) or product
(what's missing from your remote search?). 🙏
```
